Who are SlutClo?

The brand was founded by Yevheniia Kuznietsova in 2020 in the city of Kharkov.
Our goal is to make high-quality and original (yes, all images that are printed on clothes as well as their models are developed by a team of freelance designers) clothes available to the masses.
We try to support individual seamstresses and work only with masters of their craft, so our clothes may seem a little more expensive than the rest in the mass market segment. Our fabrics are not inferior to such global brands as Levi's, Champion, Nike and many others.
